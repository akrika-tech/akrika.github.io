About Us

Akrika Technologies is a forward-thinking initiative designed to empower BTech Computer Science students with real-world software development, AI/ML, and web development skills. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical education and practical industry requirements by offering internship programs, project training, and certification services.

We believe that learning should be hands-on, engaging, and guided by professionals. Our goal is to build a platform that supports students in their technical journey and helps them confidently step into the future of technology.

In the near future, we are also building powerful AI models that aim to change the world — and you can be a part of it. Together, we are shaping the next generation of technology that transforms industries and improves lives.
